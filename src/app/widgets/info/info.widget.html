Welcome to the Wolfpack RPG!

The Wolfpack RPG is a text-based RPG run by LobotJr on the LobosJr Twitch channel. The game is played by whispering commands to LobotJr, or by using this controller page to interact with the bot directly.

You earn experience and Wolfcoins by watching the stream. Once you hit level 3, you will be allowed to select a class and begin running dungeons. LobotJr will whisper you when you reach this point with instructions to select a class.

Once you have selected a class, you can begin running dungeons. You can either form a group with other chat members, or use the group finder to be automatically placed in the dungeon queue. When you complete a dungeon, you will earn additional experience and Wolfcoins, and there is a chance you can earn an item or pet as well.

To use this controller page, click on one of the icons in the toolbar at the top to open a widget.
  • Console: A direct whisper to LobotJr where you can enter commands.
  • Character: Lets you see your character class and stats.
  • Inventory: Allows you to manage and equip your items.
  • Fishing: Allows you to fish in tournaments and view the leaderboards.
  • Pet: Allows you to manage any pets you have found.
  • Info: Displays this help dialog.
